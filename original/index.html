Two Ways to Sharpen

Farmers Market Drop-Off

  1. Wrap your knives securely in a towel and bring them to the market.
  2. Drop them off at the Sharp Mom booth—quick and simple.
  3. Enjoy shopping the market while I sharpen your knives.
  4. I’ll text you as soon as your order is ready.
  5. Pick up your knives and pay by venmo/zelle/check/cash.

Sharpening service at your doorstep

  1. Text us to request an appointment.
  2. On your appointment day, you can leave your knives or tools on the porch if you won’t be home.
  3. I arrive at the scheduled time and sharpen everything on-site, usually in under an hour.
  4. When I’m finished, you get beautifully sharp blades ready to use.
  5. Payment is quick and easy by cash/venmo/zelle or check.

Minimum for a housecall is $100.

Client Love

Photo: Chaitra at Work

Hi, I’m Chaitra.

What began as a way to help my family after losing my father to cancer grew into a lifelong connection to the craft he taught me. I sharpened knives for neighbors throughout high school, carrying his lessons with me even as life pulled me toward engineering and grad school.
Years later, while living in Toronto, I had the chance to shadow a professional sharpener who introduced me to modern techniques and tools. His generosity and mentorship refined the traditional skills my father gave me and deepened my love for the craft.
I’m a mom of two, an engineer by training, and the hands behind Sharp Mom here in Santa Clara County. After returning to the Bay Area in 2024, I decided to bring my sharpening full circle and build a local, woman-owned business. If your knives are dull, I can fix that—quickly, carefully, and right at your doorstep or at the farmers market.
