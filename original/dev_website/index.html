Two Ways to Sharpen

Route-Based Pickup & Drop-Off

I pick up knives from your area one day and drop them off the next day.

How it works:

  1. Check your date above.
  2. Text us to book your pickup for that day.
  3. I pick up your knives and tools from your home.
  4. I sharpen them off-site with care and precision.
  5. Your blades are returned the very next day, sharp and ready to use.

Minimum order for route service: $35

Sharpening at your Doorstep

Premium house-call service. I come to you and sharpen on-site.

  1. Text us to request an appointment.
  2. On your appointment day, you can leave your knives or tools on the porch if you won’t be home.
  3. I arrive at the scheduled time and sharpen everything on-site, usually in under an hour.
  4. When I’m finished, you get beautifully sharp blades ready to use.
  5. Payment is quick and easy by cash/venmo/zelle or check.

Minimum for a housecall is $100.
