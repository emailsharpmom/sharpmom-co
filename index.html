About Chai

Sharpening knives since 1991

What began as a way to help my family after losing my father to cancer grew into a lifelong connection to the craft he taught me. I sharpened knives for neighbors throughout high school, carrying his lessons with me even as life pulled me toward engineering and grad school.

Years later, I had the chance to shadow a professional sharpener in Toronto who introduced me to modern techniques and tools. His mentorship refined the traditional skills my father gave me.

I'm a mom of two, an engineer by training, and the hands behind Sharp Mom Co. here in Santa Clara County. If your knives are dull, I can fix that — quickly, carefully, and right at your doorstep.
